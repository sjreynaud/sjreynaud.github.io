About Me

I’m Sidney, a clinical‑research data scientist with 10+ years of laboratory leadership and hands‑on experience building predictive models, survival analysis workflows, and interpretable ML for oncology, nephrology, psychiatry, and developmental disorders. I specialize in transforming complex clinical, genomic, and behavioral datasets into stakeholder‑ready insights using Python, R, SQL, AWS, SHAP, LIME, and modern dashboarding tools. My background in lab operations at Sasol North America gives me a strong foundation in KPI design, quality systems, metadata‑driven reporting, and reproducible analytics. I build pipelines that are clean, transparent, and easy to maintain—ensuring clinicians, researchers, and cross‑functional teams can trust and act on the results. I’m currently pursuing a PhD in Data Science, expanding my work in interpretable modeling, risk stratification, and multimodal disease‑trajectory analysis.
